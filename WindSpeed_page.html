Wind Speed and Latitude






  The wind speeds in this sample appear to be representative of the same selection observation made in the “Max Temperature and Latitude” graph, meaning more of the city sample appears to be in the Northern Hemisphere. The winds approximately +/- 5 degrees from the equator are called the “Doldrums”, this area typically has little to no winds. Winds begin mild in the equator and moves in a northerly direction and pick up speed around 30 degrees latitude as observed in this graph.
