Max Temperature and Latitude






  We learned in elementary school that the closer we get to the equator the warmer the temperature, so it is not a surprise to find a significant number of warmer cities surrounding the equator.

As expected, more of the randomly selected cities are located in the Northern Hemisphere where there is more inhabitable land. The day this data was collected it was winter in the North, so the farther north a city the colder the average temperature. What is a surprise is the number of inhabited cities with temperatures below 0 degrees which are probably cities closest to the artic circle.
